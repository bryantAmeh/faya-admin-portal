     4→1. Product Name
     5→Faya Admin Portal
     6→2. Product Purpose
     7→The Faya Admin Portal is the internal web/admin system used by Faya staff to manage the platform
     8→across different African countries.
     9→It controls:
    10→• Country-by-country operations
    11→• Country-specific compliance rules
    12→• Customer KYC review
    13→• Merchant KYB review
    14→• Merchant devices and terminals
    15→• Staff accounts
    16→• Staff departments
    17→• Staff roles
    18→• Country permissions
    19→• Audit logs
    20→• Risk monitoring
    21→• Disputes
    22→• Settlements
    23→• Support
    24→• Compliance investigations
    25→• Fraud operations
    26→• Regulatory reporting
    27→The Admin Portal must support different staff logins, different departments, and different permission
    28→levels per country.
    29→Example:
    30→A Nigeria compliance officer should be able to review Nigerian KYC/KYB cases, but not automatically
    31→access Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, or Egypt unless granted permission.
